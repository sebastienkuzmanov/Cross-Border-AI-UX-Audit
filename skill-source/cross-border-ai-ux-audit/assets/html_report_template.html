Executive Summary概要

Acme Agent has a credible underlying workflow, but its English-market surface over-promises autonomy, under-explains data handling, and has accessibility gaps that will suppress trial conversion among cautious B2B buyers and expose the product on accessibility compliance. The strongest gaps are AI expectation-setting and trust/privacy at the first decision points. With scoped-capability language, a trust panel at the upload step, accessibility fixes, and outcome-specific CTAs, the product can reach trial-ready quality for this corridor.Acme Agent 的底层工作流可信，但面向英语市场的呈现过度承诺自主性、对数据处理说明不足，且存在无障碍缺陷，会抑制谨慎型 B2B 买家的试用转化并带来无障碍合规风险。最大的差距在于 AI 预期设定与首个决策点的信任/隐私。通过有界能力表述、在上传环节加入信任面板、修复无障碍问题、以及以结果为导向的行动按钮，产品可达到该走廊下的试用就绪水平。

04

Top Risks主要风险

  1. Absolute autonomy claim ('handles the entire workflow') mismatches actual agent behavior and erodes buyer trust.绝对自主性表述（“处理整个工作流”）与智能体实际行为不符，削弱买家信任。
  2. Upload step asks for data before explaining storage, training, and retention.上传环节在未说明存储、训练与留存前即索取数据。
  3. Accessibility gaps (contrast, labels) risk excluding users and breach accessibility requirements in regulated markets.无障碍缺陷（对比度、标签）可能将部分用户排除在外，并在受监管市场违反无障碍要求。

HighAI Expectation-SettingAI 预期设定ISSUE-1
Evidence原文/行为

HighAccessibility无障碍可达性ISSUE-5
Evidence原文/行为
Primary CTA: light-grey text (#9AA0A6) on white; icon-only buttons in the upload toolbar with no text labels.

Problem问题: Low text contrast and unlabeled icon-only controls fail WCAG 2.1 AA (1.4.3 Contrast, 4.1.2 Name/Role/Value), blocking low-vision and screen-reader users.文本对比度过低、仅图标且无标签的控件不满足 WCAG 2.1 AA（1.4.3 对比度、4.1.2 名称/角色/值），阻碍低视力与读屏用户。

Why it matters影响: Beyond excluding users, accessibility is legally required in several target markets (e.g. EU EN 301 549, US ADA/Section 508); gaps risk complaints, fines, and lost enterprise deals.除排除用户外，无障碍在多个目标市场是法律要求（如欧盟 EN 301 549、美国 ADA/Section 508）；缺陷可能招致投诉、罚款并失去企业合同。

Recommendation建议: Raise text contrast to >=4.5:1, add visible text labels (or accessible names) to icon-only controls, and run a WCAG 2.1 AA pass with assistive tech.将文本对比度提升至 >=4.5:1，为仅图标控件添加可见文本标签（或可访问名称），并用辅助技术做一次 WCAG 2.1 AA 检查。

Before改写前Primary CTA: light-grey text (#9AA0A6) on white; icon-only buttons in the upload toolbar with no text labels.
After改写后 (en)Primary CTA: #1A1A1A on white (contrast 16:1); upload buttons labeled 'Upload file', 'Replace', 'Remove'.

Target-market note市场说明: For any EU-facing corridor, treat WCAG 2.1 AA as the EN 301 549 / EAA baseline and flag for accessibility-compliance review.对任何面向欧盟的走廊，将 WCAG 2.1 AA 视为 EN 301 549 / EAA 基线，并提交无障碍合规复核。

Standards对应标准: WCAG 2.1 AA: 1.4.3 Contrast WCAG 2.1 AA: 4.1.2 Name, Role, Value EN 301 549

Limitations局限性

This audit is primarily text-driven; only the pricing page was captured live (Level-2), other surfaces were reviewed as pasted text (Level-1). Full live behavior, analytics, and deeper flows were not available, so behavior-level conclusions are limited. The score is a heuristic summary computed by a deterministic rubric over expert-judged per-dimension scores, not a certification or guarantee. Playbook rules are working hypotheses requiring local validation. Findings are cross-walked to external standards (NIST AI RMF, ISO 42001, MQM, WCAG/EN 301 549) for grounding, not as a compliance opinion. AI-assisted findings require human verification before client delivery.本审计以文本为主；仅对定价页做了实时采集（L2），其他界面以粘贴文本审阅（L1）。完整实时行为、分析数据与更深流程不可得，故行为层结论有限。评分由确定性规则对专家判定的各维度分值计算而得，是启发式概览，并非认证或保证。playbook 规则为待验证的工作假设。发现项交叉对照外部标准（NIST AI RMF、ISO 42001、MQM、WCAG/EN 301 549）以增强依据，但不构成合规意见。AI 辅助发现在交付客户前需人工核验。
